ABOUT US

At Apple, we are committed to designing and delivering innovative technology that empowers people to achieve more and connect with the world. Since our founding in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, Apple has become a global leader in technology, creating revolutionary products like the Mac, iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and AirPods. We believe in the power of simplicity, design excellence, and innovation to create products that enrich lives. Our ecosystem combines seamless hardware, software, and services, delivering experiences that are intuitive, secure, and accessible to everyone. Guided by a commitment to sustainability and ethical responsibility, we strive to make a positive impact on the world. From using 100% renewable energy across our facilities to creating inclusive opportunities for all, Apple is dedicated to shaping a better future. Headquartered in Cupertino, California, our team spans the globe, united by the mission to push the boundaries of what’s possible. At Apple, we’re not just building technology; we’re building the future.

Our Mission: To create technology that enriches lives.

Our Vision: To make the world’s best products while leaving the world better than we found it.
